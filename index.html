DEVOCIÓN's story

Devoción began with a Colombian founder who was obsessed with one question: What if New Yorkers could taste Colombian coffee as fresh as the farmers do back home?

Instead of buying from big exporters, Devoción works directly with small farms across Colombia, building relationships with the farmers who grow their beans. Each batch is roasted within days of arrival — a process that typically takes months in the traditional supply chain.
